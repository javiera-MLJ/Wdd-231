Reception Schedule

The reception will begin at 12:30 PM.

Everyone must be present by 1:00 PM to welcome the couple’s grand entrance.

Travel and Stay Options

1

Travel Friday night

You can travel on Friday night and arrive Saturday morning. If you'd like, you can explore the city or wait at the temple’s waiting area until the reception. After the wedding, you can take a late-night bus or flight back with others returning the same day.

2

Help us prepare everything

If you'd like to be an active part of our day, you can travel on Friday and help us decorate or prepare details. Your help will be invaluable, and in return, we offer you free accommodation at the hostel that night.

If so, we’d appreciate you filling out the form so we can secure your stay. Please confirm before next Friday, July 4, so we can make the reservation at the hostel.

You may leave a small tip at the hostel if you wish.

Confirm Stay
3

Travel on Saturday and stay overnight

You can also travel on the wedding day and stay afterward to enjoy the celebration. In this case, you’ll need to arrange your own accommodation. There are several nearby options!

Stay Details

The hostel we’re offering is located near the reception venue. It has simple but cozy rooms, separated by gender (men and women).

Each dormitory has around 6 bunk beds (single and a half size), ideal for sharing with other guests.

Bedding (sheets, blanket, and pillow) will be provided upon arrival. Each room also has its own bathroom.
